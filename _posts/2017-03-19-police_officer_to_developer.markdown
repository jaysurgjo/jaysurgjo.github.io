Hi everyone my name is Jordan and I've came a long way in my life and journey to learning to code. I had a hard upbringing, being from Camden, NJ and from a broken home. I never had a father or a father figure. My grandfather died when I was 5 years old and he was the only father figure we had. My mom worked hard to take care of us but life isnt always good to you. I lived in over 20 places in my life. Moved around a lot to different section 8 homes.

I've always been fascinated by computers and how they worked. I was a pretty bright kid but I never had anybody to push me to achieve my goals. I learned the hard way. I didnt have regular access to a computer until I was a teen, and then it was rented and it went away pretty fast. Ive been teaching myself how to code the past year or so and I got stuck and hit a road block. But I love coding and I am a self starter and I work very hard at everything I do. I am determined to make the career change from being a police officer to a full time developer. Coding is very exciting to me and I get excited by the idea of learning more about it.

I am working fulltime overnight as well as being a single father to a lovely 7 year old boy. But I am very deteremined to learn as much as possible and study on my own as well to make the move. I wanted to finally invest in myself so that I can be happy in my chosen profession.
